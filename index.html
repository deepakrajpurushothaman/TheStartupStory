What is MeWow?

MeWow! is perfect Social Networking App for Driving/riding/traveling enthusiast! Going to racing tracks & owning an expensive car was a social symbol, a COOL factor in the past. MeWow! vision is to gamify mobility & the activity of Driving to bring a different FUN, COOL & SOCIAL factor back to Driving for digital native mobility users. MeWow! Goals includes bringing diverse value to users who use diverse mobility

Journey Till Now

@Indo-German Innovation Conference, Hannover, Germany

2015: First competation where ideas in connected mobility space were presented and MockUps created for connectivity between modes of movement and connectivity between user and mode of movement at Start-Up Weekend in Köln.

@Start-Up Weekend, KÖLN

2015: One of the ideas in connected mobility space was presented as 'Pedestrian Safety App' entering the final round of Siemens Future mobility.

@Siemens Smart Mobility Contest, Germany

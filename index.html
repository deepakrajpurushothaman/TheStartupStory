Why Use MeWow?

Current Car mobile apps such as Mercedes Me Adapter or Mercedes Me EcoCoach connects the vehicle with the user by providing information about the car and statistics about driving behaviiour or car behaviour. These apps lacks the SOCIAL and the FUN factor that is associated with ctivity of driving/riding/traveling. MeWow! is perfect Social Networking App for users of Mercedes who are enthusiastic about driving/riding/traveling.
